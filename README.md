I approach engineering from a product and business perspective. Every system I build starts with understanding the value it delivers, then working backward into architecture, specs, and execution.

- **Engineering workflow** — structured development through roadmaps, milestones, epics, and well-defined tasks. I care about how teams ship, not just what they ship.
- **Legacy modernization** — turning fragmented codebases into maintainable, scalable foundations.
- **Mentoring & hiring** — helping engineers grow and building processes that find the right people.

I use AI as a thinking partner to validate my approach, catch blind spots, and accelerate iteration.
